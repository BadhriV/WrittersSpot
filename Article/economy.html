Why Sharing Economy is the next big thing?

Sharing Economy is going to be the next big thing, let me tell you why. Imagine earning money just by sharing your products and goods, that too just for a couple of months or a couple of weeks! And this can be done in an online based community, like Airbnb, eBay, etc. Airbnb is an american vacation rental online marketplace company where they maintain and host a marketplace accessible to their consumers on its website or via application. They provide hotels for stays, Single room, Double rooms, Suite, Fancy Yacht, Beach houses, Houseboats, Villa, Castle, etc. Anything, you name it. Airbnb will give it to you. You can find these on their official websites or in their application. Likewise eBay also provides the consumers with desired products and goods. They provide the consumers with the desired product for a specified money. They can have it either temporarily or permanently, It totally depends upon the consumer. It may vary from one to another. Let me give you an example as to why Sharing Economy is going to be the next big thing. Imagine that a customer is going abroad for personal reasons, they can rent their home for a specified time frame and can get the rent money from it, or say that you are going on a one month Vacay, and you don’t want to leave your house locked for an entire month, you can rent your home on Airbnb or any other online-based sharing economy community marketplace. You are getting profited doubly! You don’t have to leave your -house locked, and at the same time you are earning money from it. Isn’t it amazing? With the help of Airbnb and other marketplace, you can sell or rent your goods for a specified time.

Gig Economy

Gig Economy is something that has to do with the labor market characterized by the prevalence of short-term contracts or freelance work as opposed to permanent jobs. These Gig Workers may be independent contractors or freelance workers, where they may do a job for some time, and if they are not satisfied with it, they can stop it anytime they want. These workers are hired by the companies to do their jobs. There are many who are in a Gig economy. There are many freelancers who are hired by companies to finish their jobs. The main thing here is that the Sharing Economy and Gig Economy is growing at a rate that you don’t even realize that you have been hit with. Consumers are getting adapted to this, and that is why they are going to be the next big thing.
